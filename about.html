Who We Are

RGPV Notes is dedicated to helping Computer Science students succeed. We provide handwritten notes, PDFs, and previous year questions to make learning easier and accessible for everyone.

Our mission is to give students a single platform for all study materials, covering all semesters and topics with carefully curated content.
